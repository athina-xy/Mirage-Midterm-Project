Characters of Mirage

Sylwen

The Exiled Elven Warrior

Once destined to join the High Council, now cast out from her homeland. With memories fractured by the Great Event, she travels the shards to uncover the truth — and her place in it.

The Crystal Warden

Guardian of the Heart

A mysterious armored spirit bound to protect the Heart of Mirage. Some say it was once mortal — others insist it is the Heart itself, given form.

The Shadow

Seeker of Truths

Neither friend nor enemy. Appears across fragments with impossible knowledge. Knows Sylwen’s name — and claims she was framed.
