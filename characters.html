Main Characters

Sylwen

The Exiled Elven Warrior

Once destined to join the High Council, now cast out from her homeland for supposedly destroying the Crysalis. With memories fractured by the Great Event, she travels the shards to uncover the truth and restore balance.

Miralyth

The Guardian

A mysterious armored spirit with teal glowing eyes, bound to protect the Shard of Nymera. Rumours say that once it was mortal, some others believe it is the Shard itself, given form.

Eidolite

Seeker Of Truth

Stuck in between fragments, Eidolite has seen many truths. Can be found in many realities and claims Sylwen was framed. Its allegiance is unclear. Its existence is a mystery. But wherever Eidolite appears, illusions tremble.
