The Fractured Realms

Long ago, Mirage was one. A world of starlit cities, crystal forests, and harmony between beings of magic and light. But when the Great Event struck, reality shattered into floating realms, each obeying different laws of nature.

Sylwen was exiled, accused of causing the disaster. Her memories are fractured, but her journey is clear: travel through the shards, uncover the truth, and restore her honor.

What if Mirage was never meant to be whole?

About Mirage

The heart of Mirage was created after the big incident that separated the reality into fragments in order to be able to contain all the new created realities, anyone that got their hands on the heart of mirage is able to manipulate the realities. Long ago, a spell shattered reality into fragments. Illusions and truth now mix freely. Sylwen must find the Heart of Mirage, but every creature, guild, and shadow being also wants its power.
