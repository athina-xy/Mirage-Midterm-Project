The Fractured Realms

Long ago, Mirage was one. A world of starlit cities, crystal forests, and harmony between beings of magic and light. But when the Great Event struck, reality shattered into floating realms, each obeying different laws of nature.

Sylwen — once a promising mage — was exiled, accused of causing the disaster. Her memories are fractured, but her journey is clear: travel through the shards, uncover the truth, and restore her honor.

What if Mirage was never meant to be whole?

About Mirage

Mirage is a task management system created by the Guild of Fractured Realms. With reality split into shards, guild members needed a powerful way to track missions, record discoveries, and maintain order.

Features:

  • Add, edit, delete, and complete tasks
  • Sort and filter by status or date
  • Summary of tasks and activity
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Dark/Light mode
  • Local storage to keep tasks saved between visits
