Storyline

Long ago, Aetherstone was whole, a world full of magic. The Crysalis kept the world balanced, and everyone shared one reality. Until the Veilburst happened. Shattering the known Crysalis into bits. The world was never the same again. The reality was split into fragments, drifting like islands in an endless void. Each fragment became its own reality, its own truth. Magic, time, and even physics could bend differently from one shard to another. The Veilburst’s nature is still unknown, since it was nothing like a spell, and definitely not an accident. Something far more powerful than the world could comprehend. Sylwen was one of Veilburst’s victims, since as a young elf, she happened to be near the burst, causing her to have memory loss. And of course, causing her to be exiled, and blamed, for the destruction of the world. To prevent total collapse, an ancient crystal formed at an unknown location. The Shard of Nymera. A relic capable of holding realities together or even erasing them forever. Some may call it salvation. Others may call it a weapon. Sylwen’s goal is to find the relic to restore balance, to hopefully find her way back home. But is everything Sylwen was taught true?

That’s up to the you to decide.

About Mirage

Mirage is a story-driven adventure game set in a world shattered into drifting realities. It blends narrative exploration, choice-based progression, and moral consequence into a branching storyline where every decision reshapes the path ahead. Players take the role of Sylwen, an exiled elven warrior whose fractured memories may hold the key to understanding the Veilburst, the catastrophe that split the world into countless realms. Through exploration of surreal environments, encounters with mysterious beings, and choices that challenge perceptions of truth, players uncover hidden histories and shape the future of Aetherstone.

Your decisions influence:
  • Sylwen's identity
  • Different realities
  • Alliances between characters
  • Oppositions between characters
  • Endings
