Summary

After the Crysalis was destroyed, causing reality to shatter into fragments, Sylwen gets exiled for contributing in the destruction of the reality.
Illusions and reality are now blend together, and Sylwen's quest is to restore balance by finding the legendary Luminite that holds all realities before it gets in the wrong hands and restore her honor.
Through her adventures, more truths are slowly uncovered about her past, since her memory is foggy after the destruction of the Crysalis.
Sylwen's journey from now on, is up to you to decide.
