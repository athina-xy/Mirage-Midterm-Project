The Fractured Realms

Long ago, Mirage was one. A world of starlit cities, crystal forests, and harmony between beings of magic and light. But the harmony was fragile — and when the Great Event struck, reality shattered.

Fragments of existence split apart. Entire lands became floating shards of space, drifting in the void. Truth and illusion blurred. Rivers flowed backwards in some realms, time froze in others.

Sylwen’s Exile

Sylwen — a young elven mage — was exiled from her homeland, accused of unleashing the catastrophe. They claimed that in her reckless attempts to help her people, she tampered with ancient forces she couldn’t control.

But Sylwen remembers little. Her memories are fractured — flashes of magic, a blinding light, then silence. The Council insists she caused the calamity. But whispers in the Realms say otherwise.

The Heart of Mirage

Legends tell of an ancient crystal — the Heart of Mirage — holding the power to restore balance. Sylwen sets out to find it, hoping to reclaim her honor and return home.

Yet as she travels through the fragmented worlds, encountering cultures, creatures, and truths that don’t align... she begins to wonder:

What if Mirage was never meant to be whole?

Perhaps each shard is a world of its own — complete, alive, and real. Perhaps truth is not singular. Perhaps balance is not what she was told.

And somewhere deep within the shards, the Heart waits — not to restore what once was, but to reveal what Mirage was always meant to be…
